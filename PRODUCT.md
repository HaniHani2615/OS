## Register
product

## Users
Vietnamese university CS/IT students (năm 1-2) preparing for the Operating Systems midterm exam. They open this on a laptop or phone, often the night before the exam, in a dorm room or library. They are stressed, want to drill efficiently, and need to trust the answers they see.

## Product Purpose
A focused study tool — question bank browser, quiz drills, mock exam simulation, and flashcards — built around the actual exam question pool. Design serves the task: get through questions, understand why answers are correct, build confidence before the test.

## Brand Personality
Focused, disciplined, trust-building. Like a well-made study app that respects the user's time. Not gamified, not corporate. It should feel like a tool a sharp classmate built — precise and useful.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS violet gradient (e.g. Vercel/Linear clone aesthetic)
- Duolingo gamification — too playful, undermines seriousness
- Google Forms — too bare and lifeless

## Strategic Design Principles
- Interface disappears into the task. No decoration that competes with question content.
- Consistent component vocabulary across all modes (quiz, flashcard, exam, read).
- Dark is the primary theme (late-night study sessions, reduces eye strain). Light mode must be fully functional, not an afterthought.
- Typography earns trust. Questions must be readable at a glance with clear hierarchy.
- State communication is critical: correct/incorrect/pending must be instant and unambiguous.
